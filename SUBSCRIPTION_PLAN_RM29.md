# RM29.90 Subscription Plan

## Recommended Offer

Price: RM29.90 per month

Best positioning: Malay short-video workspace for creators who want scripts, voice, image prompts, manual frame upload, subtitles, and export in one place.

This version should be sold as a creator tool, not a fully automatic AI video generator yet. The strongest selling point is control: users can generate script and voice, make images in ChatGPT, upload the best 4 frames, then export a ready video.

## Included In RM29.90

- 4-frame video workspace
- Niche selection
- Gemini script generation
- Gemini voice generation
- 10 Gemini voice types
- ChatGPT image prompts
- Manual 4-image upload
- Subtitle rendering
- Video preview
- MP4/WebM export

## Suggested Limits

Start with soft limits so costs stay predictable:

- 30 exported videos per month
- 120 Gemini voice clips per month
- Unlimited manual image upload
- Unlimited local previews
- No included image generation until Google image quota/cost is stable

This protects margin because image generation is the most expensive and least predictable part.

## Why RM29.90 Can Work

The price is low enough for Malaysian small creators, students, and micro sellers to try monthly. It is easier to sell if the promise is specific:

"Create Malay faceless short videos faster with script, voice, prompt, subtitle, and export tools."

Avoid promising unlimited AI image generation at this price unless you have fixed provider costs.

## Main Customer Segments

- TikTok/Reels/Shorts creators
- Small business owners
- Affiliate sellers
- Teachers or kids content creators
- Islamic reminder creators
- Story and horror niche pages

## Cost Strategy

Use bring-your-own-key for early beta users if possible. For public subscription, keep image generation manual or prompt-based first.

Suggested beta:

- RM29.90/month
- Users use your app for script, voice, prompts, upload, subtitle, export
- No included hosted AI image generation

Later upsell:

- RM59.90/month with hosted image credits
- RM99.90/month with team/export history/cloud storage
